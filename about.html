We're revolutionising the way businesses are started

Bodhivriksh Bizsol LLP is the single-source solution to a complete range of registrations, accounts, finance and human resources issues that every company confronts in today’s competitive business environment. Our talent acquisition solutions are based on our number of years of experience helping clients to maximize the performance of their Account, Finance, HR department resulting to the overall growth of the organization.

We are experts in legal, professional and financial advisory services and help our clients in setting up their business entities and managing various business functions feasibly and effectively. All our services aim at helping our clients decode the legal and regulatory requirements related to their business. Our aim is to become the ‘partner of choice’ when it comes to incorporate and managing business in India.

We ensure that each of our clients, irrespective of the size of their undertakings, receive the kind of personalized attention that is usually attributed to only the biggest clients. Our constant innovation and continued investment in new technology helps us to provide our clients with realistic, practical and cost efficient solutions.

Working with us has many benefits for you, your business and your stakeholders. We are known for our creative, yet professional approach, which is based on establishing a unique relationship with each of our clients.

Our team is comprised of experienced HR Consultants, Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Lawyers, Cost Accountants, Chartered Engineers and Bankers. Backed by great experience in our professionals assist entrepreneurs in picking the right way of setting up their business entities.
